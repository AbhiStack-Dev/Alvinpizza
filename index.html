About Us.

Alvin Pizza was founded in 2016 by Jagdeep Singh in Phase 9, Mohali, with the vision of redefining the pizza experience. Combining traditional Italian techniques with a creative flair, Jagdeep sought to bring a slice of joy to every table. His dream was to create a place where people could gather, share, and celebrate over a delicious pizza.

Alvin Pizza invites everyone to be a part of its journey—whether as a customer, franchisee, or team member. The brand values passion, creativity, and dedication, making it a rewarding experience for all involved.
