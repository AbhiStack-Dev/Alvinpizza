Our Journey

Founded in 2016, Alvin Pizza was born from a simple idea: to create pizzas that bring people together. Starting as a humble pizza shop in phase Ix Mohali, we’ve grown into a beloved brand known for our unique recipes, bold flavors, and commitment to quality. Over the years, Alvin Pizza has expanded to multiple locations, serving thousands of happy customers who keep coming back for more.
What began as a local favorite has now become a community staple, and our journey is fueled by the love and loyalty of our customers.

Our Mission

At Alvin Pizza, our mission is simple: To serve freshly made, mouthwatering pizzas crafted with love and the finest ingredients. To provide an exceptional dining experience that brings joy to every customer. To inspire communities to come together over great food. We aim to be more than just a pizza shop – we aspire to be a place where friends, families, and neighbors gather to share unforgettable moments.

What Makes Us Unique?

1. Quality Ingredients:   We believe that great pizza starts with the finest ingredients. That’s why we source the freshest vegetables, premium meats, and authentic cheeses to ensure every bite is bursting with flavor. Our dough is made fresh daily, and our sauces are crafted in-house using secret recipes passed down over the years.

2. Innovation in Every Slice:   At Alvin Pizza, we’re always exploring new flavors and combinations to excite your taste buds. From classic Margheritas to gourmet creations like BBQ Chicken or Truffle Mushroom, our menu is a perfect blend of tradition and innovation.

3. Customization Options:   We know every customer has unique preferences. That’s why we offer customizable options to suit your taste. Whether it’s gluten-free crusts, vegan cheese, or extra toppings, we’ve got something for everyone.

4. Beyond Pizza:   While pizza is at the heart of what we do, our menu also features a variety of appetizers, pastas, salads, and desserts to complement your meal. Don’t miss our signature garlic bread or indulgent chocolate lava cake!

Our Values

1. Customer-Centric Approach:   Your satisfaction is our top priority. From the first bite to the last crumb, we’re here to make your experience unforgettable.

2. Community First:   We’re proud to be an active part of the community, supporting local events, charities, and schools. Alvin Pizza isn’t just about food; it’s about building connections.

3. Sustainability:   We care about the environment and strive to reduce our carbon footprint. From eco-friendly packaging to minimizing food waste, we’re doing our part for a greener future.

4. Excellence in Service:  Our team is trained to deliver fast, friendly, and efficient service, whether you’re dining in, taking out, or ordering delivery.

Our Customer Promise

When you choose Alvin Pizza, you’re choosing:
